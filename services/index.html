Baseball Glove Relacing, Repair & Restoration Services

Services listed here are my basic services provided. If you do not see what you are looking for, please feel free to contact me using the contact link. I will do my best to accommodate you based on my current workload and ability.

*Prices shown are for standard services provided. They can vary depending on your specific glove and any extra work that may be needed. I will contact you to finalize the price before I begin any work.

Craftsman’s Certified Relacer

Murph’s Mitt Maintenance is a recognized Craftsman Certified Relacer (CCR), having completed professional relacing certification and demonstrated techniques that meet the standards established by The Craftsman’s Works.

Certification confirms verified relacing skills, proper lace selection, glove-safe preparation, and a commitment to long-term glove integrity.

Learn more about the certification program →

Standard Full Service - $80–$100

  • Thorough cleaning.
  • Conditioning with The Craftsman's Works glove lotion.
  • Full relace with lace color(s) of your choice.
  • Condition with The Craftman's Works glove salve.
  • Standard price is $80 — catchers mitts, first base mitts, trapeze, and modified trapeze webs are an additional $20.

Clean & Condition - $50

  • Clean and condition only can be performed if laces are in good shape and don’t need to be replaced.
  • Only recommended if laces are not dry, cracking, or damaged.
  • I will typically not condition without cleaning it first so dirt/contaminants aren’t present when applying conditioner.

Lace Repair - $40

  • Minor lace repair on a specified section of the glove.
  • We’ll discuss options upon receipt of service request.
  • Depending on the extent, recommendation may be made for a full relace.

Palm Padding - $20

  • Additional palm padding added in the palm (between the two layers of leather).
  • SHOCKtec® Air2Gel

Thumb / Pinky Loop Replacement - $30 ea.

  • Thumb and pinky loops can be replaced upon request.
  • Each loop is $30. This can be done on most gloves on the market.

Web Replacement - Prices Vary

  • Webs can be replaced upon request. This will increase lead time as I do not keep web replacements in stock.
  • Prices vary depending on the web type.
  • Please contact me for specific pricing.
