How It Works

Simple process. Professional results.

1. Submit Service Request

  • Request service using the Service Request button below.
  • Select the service(s) and lace color(s) you want, send photos of your glove (if you have them).
  • You will receive an email with a summary of work requested with a quote.

2. I Restore Your Glove

  • Once you agree to price and service requested, I will:
    • Disassemble the glove
    • Thoroughly clean, condition with the highest quality products from The Craftsmans Works, then relace with only the best quality leather lace from Tennessee Tannery.
    • If any other work is needed or recommended after disassembly, I will contact you before moving forward.

3. Game-Ready Return

  • You will be contacted via email that your glove is ready.
  • Payment and pick-up / shipping details will be coordinated. Gloves will not be shipped until payment is received.
  • Tracking information will be provided in the “Completed” email for shipped orders.
